Picker.wheel · DatePicker.wheels

Classic iOS wheel pickers for both units and time. No keyboard ever. DOS-y vibe. Large screen footprint.

Pros: most DOS-aesthetic, no keyboard, very native. Cons: slowest for wide ranges (2.5U requires 5+ scroll clicks), eats vertical space.

Lean: II. Stepper + quick-time chips is the shape Apple Health, Streaks, Oura, and most modern wellness apps use for frequent precise-but-small entries. Stays fully in-context (no keyboard popup), matches the chip-row vocabulary we're establishing for categories, and "⋯" always retains the native date-picker escape hatch for custom times. Keeps AddBloodGlucoseView's existing NumberSelectorView pattern (minus the slider, since insulin has a wider range than BG) as the shared primitive.
