Drill-down

Flat presets

Each chip is a fully-realised "last used / favourited" entry. Tap → staging plate prefilled. No drill-down. One tap to commit-candidate.

Chip → sheet → staging

Tap category → modal opens with presets + custom entry. Confirm in modal → hand to staging. Highest friction — also breaks "in-context" literally.

This is the current pattern for most DOSBTS entry flows today.

Recommendation: A. Two-stage drill-down matches your "expand-in-context" language literally (no modal, stays in the same row band), aligns with EventMarkerLaneView's existing shape, and scales to Meal favourites (row of tags → tap "BREAKFAST" → row of breakfast favourites → tap one → staging plate). B is faster but conflates "favourites list" with "category" — and forces every entry to be a named favourite.
